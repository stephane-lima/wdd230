Target Audience

Business owners that wants to expand their business network and increase their business exposure and revenue.

Personas

Persona 1

Gabriel Lopes is a small business entrepreneur. He is 32 years old. He is a single father with two children in elementary school. He has a degree in computer science. He started his own web design and development business 5 months ago so he could stay at home with his kids more. He wants to increase his business credibility in order to make his business grow. He spend most of his time in front a computer.

Persona 2

Laura Nascimento is the CEO and founder of a small marketing agency that provides digital marketing services to startups. She is 28 years old. She is married and she does not have chidren. She has bachelor's degree in marketing. She wants to increase her business contacts in order to attract more clients. She primarily access the web from her smartphone.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

An entrepreneur does not know much about the local business community so he uses the website to have quick access to all the latest informations about the local business community

Scenario 2

A small business owners wants to have a positive perceptions among consumer and business so he decides to join the local chamber of commerce.

Scenario 3

A small business owner has a small amount of business contacts so he uses the website to know details about up-and-coming chamber events.

Scenario 4

A small business owner wants to know more about the local chamber of commerce but does not have the time to go there in person so he try to find that information in the local chamber of commerce website.
